Photometry of Selected Eclipsing Binary Systems


According to our research, there are still a lot of relatively bright EB systems that don't have any published solution of physical parameters. That's why in 2019 Laboratory of Space Research in cooperation with the Department of Theoretical Physics and Astrophysics of UPJŠ University in Košice start a program aimed to explore such EB systems in any possible way (photometry, spectrometry, period analysis etc.) and publish the results. For these purposes we will also use photometrical data from all ground based surveys (SWASP, ASAS-N, etc.) and spase missions (KEPLER, TESS, ets.).
