Welcome to LSR

The Laboratory of Space Research of Uzhhorod National University is studying near-Earth space. We observe artificial satellites of the Earth, small bodies of the solar system, variable stars.

LSR on wikipedia
LSR on Uzhhorod National University web site

Telescope TPL-1M - designed as laser ranging system (D = 1 m, f = 11.6 m). Mounted in Derenivka in year 2000.
Now used with QHY-174M-GPS CMOS camera (installed on guider) for photometry of LEO satellites.

Telescope ChV400 - Newton with corrector (D = 400 mm, f = 1750 mm). Used with FLI P9000 CCD camera for astrometry and photometry of GEO satellites, photometry of variable stars and asteroids. Equipped with BVR filters.

Telescope BRC250M - (D = 250 mm, f = 1268 mm). Used with Alta F16M CCD camera for astrometry of GEO satellites.
